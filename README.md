# HUKUM 👑

### Artisanal Luxury • Handcrafted Excellence • Timeless Elegance

HUKUM is a premium Indian handicraft and luxury gifting platform that celebrates traditional craftsmanship through a modern digital experience.

Our mission is to bring handcrafted luxury products, artisan-made décor, spiritual collections, corporate gifting solutions, and personalized premium gifts to customers worldwide.

---

## ✨ Vision

To become India's most trusted luxury handicraft marketplace by empowering artisans and delivering timeless handcrafted products to modern consumers.
